1. Introduction

Welcome to EX-Ray. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when using our app.

2. Data We Process

Visual Evidence: Screenshots you upload are transmitted via encrypted channels (TLS/SSL) to Google Gemini API for real-time analysis. We do not store the original image files on our permanent servers once the analysis is complete.

Profile Data: We store your name, partner's name, and relationship context to maintain your personal history and provide a tailored experience.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share the text and images you provide with Google Gemini AI for processing. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers. All data processing is done under strict security protocols to ensure your information remains confidential.

4. Account Deletion & Data Request

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time. You can delete your account directly through the "Profile" section in the app.

Alternatively, users can request full account and data deletion by sending an email to markoandrijevic27@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

Upon request, all associated data, including account info, analysis history, and references to uploaded images, will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard encryption to protect your data during transmission and at rest. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.
